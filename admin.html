How to Use This Page

Add a painting: Click the "+ Add New Painting" button. Fill in the title, add a photo, set the price, and click Save.

Edit a painting: Click the "Edit" button on any painting card to change its details.

Mark as sold: Click "Mark Sold" when a painting has been purchased. It will still appear in the gallery with a "Sold" badge.

Delete a painting: Click "Delete" to permanently remove a painting from the gallery.

Backup your data: Click "Download Backup" to save a copy of all your paintings data. You can restore it later with "Restore Backup".

Connecting Shopify for Payments

To accept online payments, you need a Shopify Starter Plan. Once set up:

  1. Add each painting as a product in your Shopify admin
  2. Copy the Product ID from Shopify
  3. Paste it into the "Shopify Product ID" field when editing a painting here
  4. Ask your developer to update the Shopify configuration in js/gallery.js

Until Shopify is connected, the "Buy Now" button will open an email to you so buyers can contact you directly.
