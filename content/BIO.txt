They say life begins at forty, but for me, the real magic started at sixty.
Living on the Gold Coast , I've always been surrounded by the kind of beauty that demands to be captured. As a long -time ESL teacher, I 've helped others find their words: as a pianist , I've found peace in rhythm. But it wasn't until I picked up a brush that I found my getaway. My art is a blend of acrylics and mixed media, leaning into expressionism to tell stories that words cannot reach. I built layers that reflect a lifetime of seeking beauty. Through semi-abstract forms , I invite you to see the world not just as it is but as it feels. 
For me, every canvas is a place where the discipline of a teacher meets the rhythmic soul of a musician.
My works have been displayed at the local galleries such as " Gallery 8 " and " Artist Hub " .
